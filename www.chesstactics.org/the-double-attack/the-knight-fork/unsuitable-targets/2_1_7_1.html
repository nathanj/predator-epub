Unsuitable Targets.

Sometimes a knight fork is available but only one of the forkable pieces is a good target; the other is a piece of equal or lesser value to your knight, and perhaps is guarded. The question then is whether the bad target can be made a good one through an exchange.

We start here with a case where at first glance there might seem to be no forking possibility for Black. The point to see is that Nc5 is a potential knight fork even though it won't work now because one of its targets is another knight (so Black's Nc5+ is simply met with NxN). Ask whether you can make your opponent replace the knight with something better by forcing an exchange.
